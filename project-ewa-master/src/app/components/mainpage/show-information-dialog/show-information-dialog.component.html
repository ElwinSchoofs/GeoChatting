What is the goal of the game?

The goal of the game is to get as much points as possible. You will be doing this by opening message from other people. These messages can contain for example a joke or a funny fact. If you're the first person to open the message you will get 10 points, if you're the seconde one you will get 9 points. Besides getting points it's also nice to leave the house and play a game.

Create an account

On the right top of the page you can see the button "sign-in" once you clicked on that, you can log-in if you already have a account, if this isn't the case then click on register. On the register form you have to leave your email, nickname and your password.

create a post

At the right-bottom once you logged in you can see a blue bottom within there a icon of a message. You have to click on the button and type in your message. Your message must at least have 10 characters otherwise it won't be able to be shared. If you happen to not want to post your message, just click the cancel button and you will return to the main page. Otherwise click the button create post and your message will be shared.
